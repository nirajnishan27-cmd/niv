📸 DROP YOUR VILLA PHOTOS HERE
================================

Place your photos in this folder with EXACTLY these filenames:

  hero.jpg        → Full villa exterior / pool / aerial shot (used as the big hero background)
  about-main.jpg  → Interior or garden photo (large photo in the About section)
  about-pool.jpg  → Pool or terrace photo (small overlapping photo in About section)
  gallery-1.jpg   → Best overall shot — pool, terrace, or villa view (appears LARGE on left)
  gallery-2.jpg   → Bedroom
  gallery-3.jpg   → Kitchen or dining area
  gallery-4.jpg   → Bathroom or spa
  gallery-5.jpg   → Garden, outdoor, or another room

TIPS:
  • Use JPG format for all photos
  • Recommended size: at least 1200×800px
  • Keep each file under 500KB (compress at squoosh.app or tinypng.com)
  • hero.jpg should ideally be 1800×1000px or larger

After adding photos, run:  npm run dev
Then open:  http://localhost:3000
